Why Rum?

Rum naturally matches beach culture and “weekend escape” energy.

  • Rum brings a real sense of vacation—no need to fake the vibe.
  • Jamaican rum has personality and depth (not “neutral spirit” bland).
  • A clear category gap: rum is underrepresented in RTDs.

The Data

Gen Z shows strong curiosity around rum—creating space for a premium leader.

  • 50% Enthusiast Rating: Rum has the highest “enthusiast” interest among Gen Z.
  • Premium opening: Consumers want to learn more about rum vs other spirits.
